Welcome to Linda's Cafe

Since opening our doors, Linda's Cafe has been serving the Lexington Park community with authentic American comfort food. We believe in using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create meals that feel like home. Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast before work or enjoying a leisurely lunch with friends, our friendly staff makes you feel like family.
