Our Story

A Lexington Park Tradition

Crafted with Love, Served with Joy

Since 2010, Linda's Cafe has been the heart of Lexington Park's breakfast scene. What started as a small family diner has become a beloved community gathering place.

Every dish is made from scratch using locally-sourced ingredients. Our recipes, passed down through generations, bring authentic American comfort to your table.
